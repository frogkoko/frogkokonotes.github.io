About Thai Consonants

Thai has 44 consonants, but only 21 distinct sounds. Consonants are divided into three classes (high, mid, low) which determine tone rules.

    About Thai Vowels

    Thai has 32 vowels, consisting of short and long pairs. Vowels can appear before, after, above, or below consonants.

      About Thai Tones

      Thai is a tonal language with five tones: mid, low, falling, high, and rising.

      Tone is determined by consonant class, vowel length, syllable type, and tone marks.
